About Simrik Jutta Udhyog

Established in the foothills of the Himalayas, Simrik Jutta Udhyog crafts high-quality shoes using traditional Nepali techniques and premium leather. From juttas worn during festivals to rugged boots for trekkers, our products blend heritage with modern comfort. Proudly made in Nepal for the world.
